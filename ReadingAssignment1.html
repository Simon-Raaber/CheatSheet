Cheatsheet

World Wide Web
  1. What are the most essential technologies that made the World Wide Web such a success?
    The Universal Document Identifier UID or later know as Uniform Resource Loader URL and Uniform Resource Identifier URI.
    The Hypertext Markup Language HTML.
    The Hypertext Transfer Protocol HTTP.
  2. How are resources (HTML pages, images, etc) addressed in the web?
    Resources are adressed with the Uniform Resource Loader.
  3. What are the general components of a URL?
    The general components of an URL are Protocol, Userinfo, Host, Port, Path, Query, #Fragment.
